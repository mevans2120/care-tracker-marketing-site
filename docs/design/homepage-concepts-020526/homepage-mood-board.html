Post Pal Homepage Mood Board

Visual exploration across three distinct design directions for the Post Pal marketing homepage. Each direction takes a different strategic approach to content, tone, and aesthetic.

February 5, 2026

Three Directions

Each concept explores a meaningfully different approach to information architecture, visual tone, and conversion strategy.

Concept 1

Warm Editorial

Empathy-first storytelling with serif headlines, warm photography, and a narrative layout. Leads with emotional pain-point validation before revealing the product. Feels like a trusted health publication, not a tech product.

Serif headlines Warm tones Story-driven Light theme
Concept 2

Bold Product-Forward

Dark, immersive canvas that puts the product UI center stage with glowing, elevated mockups. AI-forward messaging positions Post Pal as cutting-edge health technology. High contrast, cinematic feel.

Dark theme Glowing UI AI-forward Cinematic
Concept 3

Clean Conversion Engine

Light, structured, stats-driven design optimized for conversion. Clear visual hierarchy with numbered steps, metric callouts, and repeated CTAs. Modern SaaS landing page energy adapted for healthcare warmth.

Light + airy Stats-driven Structured High conversion

Motion & Animation

Each concept uses a different motion personality to reinforce its design direction.

Concept 1: Gentle Fades

Slow, gentle fade-ups (600ms ease-out). Content appears like turning pages. Minimal movement, maximum grace.

Concept 2: Dramatic Reveals

Scroll-triggered parallax layers, glowing pulse effects, staggered element entrances with spring physics.

Pulse + glow effects

Concept 3: Crisp Transitions

Quick, purposeful (300ms ease). Counter animations for stats, smooth tab switches, subtle hover lifts.
